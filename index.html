About

Hi, My name is Moshe, From Petah Tikva, Israel
And I'm an UX/UI designer

I love to design websites and mobile apps, by standing the right rules. One of them is to focus on the specific target market that the product serves for them.
Which means, every time I want to define something new to the customers. I share my idea with co-workers, to be sure if there is something I can add or replace or remove before I start to research.

I also love to learn a lot by myself, whether it's through taking professional courses from the internet or learn new methods about Photoshop, XD, Figma and other design tools through #short videos from YouTube.

When I'm not designing, I play video games and like to listen to music which my favorites artists are: Gorgon City, Camelphat & David Guetta
