About Me

moshe.mauzkop@gmail.com

Hi, My name is Moshe, I'm an UX / UI Designer, from Petah Tikva.

I started to design 3 years ago. I design for the digital platforms: Mobile devices, Tablets & Desktops. I create landing pages, websites and mobile applications by using my tools and knowledge to achieve the best result.

I think design is the key to connecting people to the products that serve us every day, The Idea that can change our lives. or The Brand that wants to succeed. Design is essential for everything. In anytime.

I also love to learn new things by myself, whether it's through taking professional courses from the internet or learning new methods on Photoshop, XD, Figma and other design tools through #short videos from YouTube.
